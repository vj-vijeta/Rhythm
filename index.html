AI Orchestrator Details

The Rule of 4: The AI orchestrates changes strictly on 4, 8, and 16-bar boundaries.
Turnarounds: At the end of every 4th or 8th bar, drums/bass will cut out to create a structural drop.
Macro Timbres: Repetitive genres (Techno/Acid) lock their note loops, while the AI continuously shifts their Cutoffs, Delays, and Envelopes over 16 bars.
Slow Canvas Scroll: Images now represent a massive 8-bar block (128 steps) allowing for vast, evolving tracks instead of fast glitching loops.
